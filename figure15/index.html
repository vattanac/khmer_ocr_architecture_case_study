Figure 15. Full chunking & merging pipeline for long Khmer textlines. Each chunk (blue = chunk 1, green = chunk 2) is processed independently through the CNN → PATCHENC → TRENC stack, producing contextual feature sequences Ti ∈ ℝD. The Merging Module combines these sequences and applies positional encoding to produce merged T. The merged T together with a context sequence Z are fed to TRDEC, which autoregressively generates the predicted character sequence Ŷi ∈ ℝC.
